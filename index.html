Privacy Policy for CrediPilot

Effective Date: June 21, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how CrediPilot ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

  • Anonymous Authentication: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication to provide app functionality without requiring personal account creation
  • Financial Data: Income and expense information you enter into the app
  • Usage Data: App interaction data for improving user experience

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Analytics Data: App usage patterns and performance metrics
  • Advertising Data: Information collected by AdMob for displaying relevant ads

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Process and store your financial tracking data
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements through AdMob
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Cloud Storage: Your data is securely stored in Google Firestore
  • Encryption: All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • Access Control: Only you can access your financial data through your anonymous user ID

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and analytics
  • AdMob (Google): Advertisement serving for free users
  • App Store Connect (Apple): App distribution and subscription management

5. Advertising

Free users will see advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers and usage information to show relevant ads. Premium subscribers will not see advertisements.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal financial information with third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and users' safety
  • Anonymous, aggregated data for analytics purposes

7. Data Retention

  • Your financial data is retained as long as you use the app
  • If you delete the app, your data will be automatically deleted from our servers
  • Anonymous usage analytics may be retained for service improvement

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of personalized advertising
  • Contact us about privacy concerns

9. Children's Privacy

CrediPilot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. International Users

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Effective Date."

12. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: support@credipilot.com
  • App: Use the "Contact Support" feature in CrediPilot
